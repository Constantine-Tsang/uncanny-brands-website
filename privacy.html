Privacy Policy

Pivan Interactive, Inc. — uncanny.gg

Pivan Interactive outlines in this Privacy Policy how we collect, use, secure, and share user information across our websites, software, and applications (collectively, the "Services").

Information We Collect or Receive

Pivan Interactive collects personal data with an appropriate legal basis, including through user provision, automatic collection via cookies and servers, and information from third parties. We process data for service delivery, legal compliance, and legitimate interests like fraud prevention and analytics.

1. Information You Voluntarily Choose to Provide

Users may share personal details when creating accounts, making purchases, entering competitions, or contacting support. Information collected may include your name, display name, email address(es), and payment details for transactions. Job applicants may also provide phone numbers, addresses, and dates of birth.

2. Information Collected Automatically When You Use Our Services

We automatically gather technical data about devices and usage patterns. This includes IP addresses, browser information, URLs of our websites that you have visited, page viewing duration, and crash reports. This data serves purposes like feature improvement and fraud prevention. We use cookies, log files, web beacons, and our back-end servers to collect this information.

3. Information We Receive from Others

Third parties, including social networks and gaming platforms, may provide user information to Pivan Interactive. We collect data when users access software through third-party platforms or utilize social features, obtaining details like profile pictures and connection identifiers.

How We Use and Share Information

Data usage supports service provision, user understanding, personalization, product development, advertising management, and security. We retain data for as long as we reasonably need it to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

Information may be shared with:

  • Our subsidiaries and affiliates
  • Service providers who assist in operating our Services
  • Advertisers and advertising networks
  • Social network contacts when you use social features
  • Law enforcement authorities when legally required or to prevent harm

Your Choices

You have several options to control information sharing and provision:

  • Browse without providing personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Adjust your social network privacy settings
  • Manage cookies and tracking preferences through your browser settings
  • Limit interest-based advertising through opt-out portals

Access and Correction

You can request access to and correction of your personal information. You may update account details online or submit email requests to contact@uncanny.gg with your identifying information and specific correction requests.

How We Protect Personal Information

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Users must keep passwords confidential to maintain account security.

Children's Privacy

Pivan Interactive does not intentionally collect data from children under 13. Parents who believe their child's information was collected should contact us at contact@uncanny.gg for deletion.

Transfers of Information

Personal data may be transferred internationally to our subsidiaries, affiliates, and partners. While foreign jurisdictions may offer different levels of protection than your home country, we apply consistent safeguards to your information regardless of where it is processed.

Additional Privacy Rights

EU residents may have additional rights including the ability to restrict or object to processing, prevent marketing communications, and request data portability under certain conditions. Deletion and access rights exist with specified limitations regarding other individuals' information and legal claim defense.

Updates to Our Privacy Policy

This policy undergoes periodic updates reflecting changing practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this policy regularly when accessing our Services to stay informed about how we protect your information.

How to Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at contact@uncanny.gg with your name, contact information, and the details of your request.
